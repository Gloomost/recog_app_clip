Get ready for some classic and easy-breezy fun! All you gotta do is tap to clear the stars. Just match up two stars of the same color that are next to each other and they're outta there! The more stars you clear in one shot, the higher your score goes. Hit certain score levels and you'll unlock a nifty multiplier cube. Just tap it and watch your score get doubled, baby! Keep pushing yourself to beat your own high score and become a star of the game!
