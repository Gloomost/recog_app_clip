Prepare for the ultimate puzzle challenge in Snake Master! Tap and release the snakes to set them free. Can you become the master of puzzle-solving and guide all the snakes to their freedom?
In this captivating game, strategically tap on the snakes to untangle them. Each level presents interlocked snakes that must be released in the correct order. As you progress, the puzzles become more complex, testing your observation and planning skills.
Beware of treacherous traps along the snake's path! Avoid them at all costs, as one misstep means game over. Stay alert and plan a safe route for the snakes to reach their freedom.
Boss levels await you, introducing time limits and limited moves. Put your puzzle-solving skills to the test and find the best solution within these constraints. Completing boss levels rewards you with extra money to unlock new features.
