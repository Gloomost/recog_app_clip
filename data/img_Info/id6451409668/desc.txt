Struggling with what to eat every day?
Having trouble making a choice?
Don't know what to eat today?
 
Now Random Pik can help you！One-click and random choose！
 
You can add all kinds of foods you like to the choose pool, and you can give them a note tag for your favorite food. When you choose, you will randomly select a food blind box. Sounds pretty good? What if I choose food that I don't want to eat or can't eat today?Then choose again！
 
At Random Pik, you can add a diet log freely, and Random Pik will automatically calculate calories and energy for you. You can also add your height, weight, and expected weight, and Random Pik will help you calculate your daily weight curve to help you control your weight healthily.
