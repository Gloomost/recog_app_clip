Defend your land! Merge and Build your army to defeat powerful enemies!

North Tower is a good quality tower defense 3D game, which combined merge and clicker gameplay with idle game features.
Players need to defeat enemies by Merge weapons of the same level to power them up, grow army to clear all the enemies.
This is also a classic idle tower defense game, clear all the enemies and guard the center tower with the surrounded turrets and Heroes.
