Welcome to Lucy Ball: Crazy Day! If you're tired of playing the traditional pinball game, you should definitely give this new gameplay a try!

How to play? Simply choose a number, then throw balls and complete levels! This game guarantees endless fun and relaxation without the fear of losing. You can throw the balls in any way you want, launch multiple balls and observe their trajectory. There are no limits to how you can play! See if you can set a new record!

Every ball drop in this game brings an exhilarating experience. Watch as the ball bounces and zooms through a maze of pins, creating a mesmerizing display of lights and sounds. Will it land in a high-scoring pocket? The suspense adds to the excitement!

Lucy Ball: Crazy Day is completely free for all players. Any winnings you accumulate are virtual coins that cannot be exchanged for real money. It is not possible to deposit or withdraw real money in this game. Achievements in the game have no impact on your real-life situation.

So what are you waiting for? Get ready to have a blast with Lucy Ball: Crazy Day!
