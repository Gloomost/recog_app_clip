Embrace the thrill of archery in Archer: Shooting Stars, an engaging mobile game that will test your aim and precision.Features:Simple and intuitive controls: Simply tap left or right buttons to aim and shoot button to shoot.Strategic challenge: With only seven arrows to work with, you'll need to be precise and strategic to reach the goal of five stars.Gameplay:Aim your arrows by tapping left or right buttons.Shoot button to shoot and hit the stars.Earn points for each star you hit.Reach the goal of five stars to win the game.Tips:Be patient and wait for the perfect moment to shoot.Don't waste your arrows!
