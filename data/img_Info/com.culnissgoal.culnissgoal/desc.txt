Zelivonic is an application that focuses on helping users grow, where you can develop your OKR, todo list, future lettersFeatures-Make OKR, a set of management tools and methods to clarify your goals and their completion, set OKR management for yourself, you can make your goals more clear-Lightweight todo, where you can easily and quickly set small tasks, complete and delete it-Future letter, you can write a letter for your future self, you can open it years later to see if the goals and ideals you once set are achieved-We also provide a weather system, where you can easily check the weather of the dayMore small features will be added in the future, so come and experience it!
