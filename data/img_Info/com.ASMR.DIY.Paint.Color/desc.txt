Step into the colorful and imaginative world of Paint Master! Challenge yourself with an array of painting puzzles created by the secret host. Your task is simple - arrange the painters in the correct order to match the given drawing. Become a paint master by carefully selecting the colors and order of the painters to create an exact replica of the drawing. Any deviations from the original will send you back to the start, so put your skills to the test. How to Play:- Observe the given drawing- Strategize the best order for the painters- Tap to start painting- Tap again to undo your previous move- Complete your masterpiece by getting all the painters to match the drawing
