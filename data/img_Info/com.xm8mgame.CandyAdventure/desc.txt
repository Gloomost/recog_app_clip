This is an elimination game called "CandyAdventure". Players need to eliminate stones of the same color to earn points, advance to the next level and unlock more magic items. The game interface consists of an array of gems, players can choose any two adjacent gems to swap places, if three or more of the same gems are aligned, they will disappear and score points. Different colored stones have different special effects, such as red stones can detonate surrounding stones, blue stones can freeze surrounding stones, green stones can make you gain extra steps and so on. As the game progresses, the difficulty increases, requiring the player to plan each move and use items more skillfully. The game has a variety of levels and modes, players can choose to challenge different difficulties and experience different types of magic items according to their preferences. Challenging your reaction speed and thinking ability, Magic Game will give players fun and challenge, and can be easily enjoyed in fragmented time.
