Marble Quest is a marble shoot game with the goal to clear all the marbles before they reach the end of the path. It is relaxing and exciting. Pinball style classic well-designed levels with backgrounds for multiple topics.Try your best to achieve marbles and combos as many as you can to get a higher score.Find the right angle and aim at the place where you want to shoot the marbles.Match 3 or more same color marbles to make a blast.Swap the shooting marble by touching the marble emitter.
