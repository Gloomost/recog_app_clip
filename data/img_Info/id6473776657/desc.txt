Legend of the Secret Treasure Maze is an immersive legendary game that transports you to a mysterious and ancient world. As a brave adventurer, you will navigate through a dangerous forest teeming with monsters in search of a forgotten city. Conquer dragons, defeat trolls, solve intricate puzzles, and uncover the secrets hidden deep within the maze to claim unimaginable treasures.

Gameplay features:

Mystic world. Grand map to be explored. Unlock new unknown areas.

Different kinds of monsters. You'll meet plenty of monsters along the journey. Defeat them and grab their loots!

Abundant character classes. Mysterious Taoist, brave Warrior, and marvelous Wizard are your faithful teammates.

Epic Divine power system. Explore and unlock the Divine power feature. The power would be a great assistance.

Mighty Mount and Pet system. You won't be lonely during the adventure if you have reliable mount and pet fighting with you together. They will be light of the dawn when you are facing the darkness.

The game will make immerse yourself in a world full of adventure and unknown. You will challenge mysteries, defeat formidable enemies, and truly feel an amazing adventure while tracking the lost treasure!
