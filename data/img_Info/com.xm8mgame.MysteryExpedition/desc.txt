Players need to eliminate blocks of the same pattern or color to earn points and advance to the next level. The game interface consists of an array of blocks, players can choose any two adjacent blocks to swap places, if three or more of the same blocks are aligned, they will disappear and score points. As the game progresses, the difficulty increases, requiring the player to plan each move more skillfully. The game has a variety of levels and modes, and players can choose to challenge different difficulties according to their preferences. Challenging your reaction speed and thinking ability, eliminating games gives players fun and challenges, and can be easily enjoyed in fragmented time.
