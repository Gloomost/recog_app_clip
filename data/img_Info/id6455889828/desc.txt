Welcome to Island Farm Adventure, a farming adventure game that you can harvest various crops, explore mysterious islands and grow your own island. Embark on your own rafting adventure, discover new companions and defeat your enemies between different fantasy islands.

Expand territory
Expand your island, collect various materials, expand your island area and build a variety of different buildings.

Explore new island
Leave your island and explore the mysterious tropical island with your captain, help new comrades in the island, and defeat the enemies you encounter.

Unlock elements
Unlock over 100 free building materials and ships, unlock dozens of characters in different shapes, and unlock and land on surviving islands around the world

Grow the team
Save those who survived the sea, the survivors will be your crew. The more crew you have, the stronger you will be in the sea.

Agriculture
Start farm on your own island. Use your cooking skills to harvest crops, raise farm animals and produce food. Turn your farm in this simulator into a culinary powerhouse.

Upgrade your crew, arrange various jobs for them, and get busy in order to make your island prosperous.

Island Farm Adventure is free to play and will always be free to play. Some in-game items can be purchased with money. This will help speed up the progress in the game but are not mandatory to participate in any of the content.
