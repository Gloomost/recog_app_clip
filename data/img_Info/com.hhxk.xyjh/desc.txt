The beautiful martial arts game "Xiaoyao Jianghu" leads players to gain an in-depth understanding of the grievances and hatreds in the world of immortal warriors through a fascinating plot. In addition, the game also designs rich and colorful mounts, immortal pets, forgings, god-king transformations, realms, etc. for players. The combat power improvement system, diversified interactive systems and gameplay such as chatting, gangs, and marriages add new challenges and fun to the game. Players can work side by side with their friends in this fairy tale world full of strong oriental colors, strive for progress in the game together, and explore the mysterious and unknown fantasy continent.
