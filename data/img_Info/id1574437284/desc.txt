
Flowers Island is a casual garden game that will help you relax. All you need is to plant flowers in the field. The shop will offer or sell more precious flowers. And you can get more flowers by upgrading your level.
Create your own flower shop. If you like beautiful flowers, this is the best game for you!

How To Play
Hundreds of flowers for you to plant and decorate your garden
Unleash imagination to arrange flowers the way you like
Furniture with various styles help you design a unique flower shop
Interaction system, community events, planting research, etc. Rich creative gameplays are waiting for you to explore.
