Sarah is a scientist. She often develops some new potions at her home in the deep sea. In an accident, the small fish she raised accidentally ate the unfinished potions and all turned to stone. Sarah was so sad that she could not sleep all night. Sleeping day and night, she thought about her little fish, so she began to develop a potion to save the little fish. Today, she finally developed this magical potion. Now let us help Sarah wake up these little fishes!
