At the beginning of the game, the player needs to use three or more identical items appearing in the game screen to eliminate, and each successful elimination can obtain the corresponding gold and experience points. As the number of eliminations increases, the difficulty of the game increases, requiring the player to use their skills and strategies more intelligently and flexibly. This is a challenging and fun game, both treasure hunters and ordinary players can find their own fun and enjoyment in it. Let's enter the mysterious world of Persia together and start a wonderful elimination journey!
