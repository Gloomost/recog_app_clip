Players will play the role of a manipulator of a mechanical dragon, defeating invaders in different scenarios, including mutated zombies, demons, magical creatures, etc. by manipulating the mechanical dragon.Each scene has different terrain and defenses. Players need to choose different weapons and defense measures to fight according to the terrain and enemy characteristics.In combat, mechanical dragons can launch rockets and lasers, release electromagnetic pulses and flames, and prevent enemies from approaching the base.As the game progresses, players will receive coins and energy, which can be used to purchase new weapons and defense measures. Energy can be used to upgrade the skills and attributes of mechanical dragons.At the same time, players need to collect resources such as minerals and energy crystals to upgrade their base and cultivate stronger mechanical dragons.
