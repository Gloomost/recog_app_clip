Game Introduction:This is a shooting game with a sniper as the main character. In this game, players will play a brave sniper, facing a huge monster that is wantonly destroying the city. Your mission is to protect the residents of the city and prevent the destructive behavior of monsters.Game features:1. Exciting shooting experience: Enjoy a realistic shooting experience through highly restored sniper shots, feeling the thrill of bullets passing through the air and the satisfaction of hitting monsters.2. Unique mission scenario: Engage in battles in multiple different urban scenarios, from bustling cities to abandoned cities filled with ruins, and experience different combat environments.3. Powerful weapon system: unlock and upgrade various Sniper rifle and special weapons, select the most suitable equipment to fight against monsters and defeat them.4. Accurate shooting skills: By practicing and improving skills, become an accurate shooter and use sniper guns to accurately strike the weaknesses of monsters.
