In Draw 4 Battle: Aim N Fight, you will fight 1v1 with the enemy at close range. All you need to do is to draw the attack line continuously, and use your hands and feet to make the most handsome and silky combos to kill your opponent. defeat.Only by emptying the opponent's health bar or hitting the water is a victory. Of course, if your health bar is empty or thrown into the water, you will lose. Sometimes there will be cars or trains crashing over, and even the battle between you and your opponent will appear on the plane, and you can use the scene cleverly to defeat your opponent.When you defeat an enemy, don't take it lightly, because there may be a second or third enemy, and you can only pass the level if you defeat all the enemies.
