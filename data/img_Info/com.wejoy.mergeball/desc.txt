On the distant planet Bobo, there lives a group of cute ball elves. They have different personalities but the same love for sports and competition. They look cute but they all have the unique ability to change their size.On the planet Bobo, which is full of love and peace, the most popular way of exercising is "big balls eating small balls", and the most popular competitive mode is the limited-time competition. In Ball Evolution, players will play the role of a ball elf to participate. Enter their limited-time competition and try to win the first place by eating small balls with big balls, so that your Ball Elf can become the well-deserved "Light of Bobo".
