Easy to use QR reader, ideal application for quick scanning of QR codes and barcodes!
The application contains the following functions:
- Can scan the product QR code to obtain the content information.
- The product bar code can be scanned to obtain content information.
- Can scan the QR code or bar code in the picture.
- You can create your own QR standard code, including Email, phone, URL, text, etc.
- Can create special codes that include Facebook, Instagram, Linkedin, Twitter, Youtube, App Store, etc...
