Dimension Hunter is a Roguelike+TPS multiplayer game where you need to master positioning skills and become proficient in utilizing the active abilities of various heroes. You must also excel at wearing different types of armor and mastering various types of weapons. By crafting powerful equipment, choosing different paths in dungeons, and fearlessly confronting vicious monsters, you can acquire abundant skill items. Combine them with weapon upgrades to create a formidable set of tactics, defeat bosses, and explore new chapters.
