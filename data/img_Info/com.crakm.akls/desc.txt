The human who can capture and summon the elves in the magic capsule is called the elf summoner.In the game, you will play the role of an elf summoner and have 6 magic capsules that can capture any elf.Just take them to embark on an adventurous journey.The elves captured will be your companions, and you will take your companions to fight and take risks.Get stronger and stronger,catch more elves!Now,take your elves and go on an adventure!
