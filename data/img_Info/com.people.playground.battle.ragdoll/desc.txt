People Fight Playground is a fun and wacky 2D arcade fighting game with realistic physics. Battle as a variety of characters, from kids to adults, in a colorful playground. The game is easy to pick up and play, with simple controls and tons of combos.In People Fight Playground, you can experience thrilling and engaging fighting matches with realistic physics. The characters in the game have weight and movement physics that are just like real life. This means that your punches and kicks will have real impact, and you can use the environment to your advantage.For example, you can knock your opponent into a wall, trap them under a slide, or even send them flying through the air. The possibilities are endless!
