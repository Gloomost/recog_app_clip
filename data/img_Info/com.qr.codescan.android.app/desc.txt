QR Code Scan is a convenient and fast mobile application designed to help users quickly scan and create two-dimensional codes (QR codes) to obtain relevant information.Main feature:1.Fast scanning: QR code scanning assistant has an efficient scanning engine, which can quickly capture and recognize the QR code on the screen or in front of the camera lens.2.Has historical scanning and creation functions3.Very easy to use, just enter the data you want and click to generate a QR code.
