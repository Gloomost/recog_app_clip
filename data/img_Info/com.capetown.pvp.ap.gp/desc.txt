‘Brave Heroes’ is a turn-based strategy chess mobile game. Taking the imaginative Romance of the Three Kingdoms as its theme, it innovatively blends elements of gods, demons, and mystical creatures, making each faction and character's attributes even more prominent.Players will embody a rising lord of a power in this chaotic era, forming their ideal army based on the cards in their possession. Command your troops and generals, utilizing their skills, to face off against friends and foes alike in exciting battles.With nearly 200 cards, six major factions, four Lord classes, and dozens of genre combinations, this game offers a high level of strategy and fun in real-time player matching, either for battles or team play.The gameplay and event features are simple and pure. The turn-based strategy and auto battles can provide players with a relaxing yet competition charm.
