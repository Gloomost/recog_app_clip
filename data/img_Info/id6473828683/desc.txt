"Find It" is an adventure puzzle game where players need to find hidden objects and clues in various scenes in the game to solve puzzles and advance the game.

Gameplay: Players need to play a cute little detective in the game and solve puzzles and advance the story by finding hidden objects and clues in the magnificent world.
Scene settings: The scene settings in the game are diverse and beautiful, from suburban cities to charming Japanese gardens and mysterious manor, each scene has a unique style and atmosphere.
Find items and clues: In the game, players need to find hidden items and clues through observation and reasoning. Some items can be very difficult to find, requiring players to search and think carefully.
Storyline: The game has a colorful storyline involving multiple characters and events. Players need to solve puzzles and reason to understand the full story and reveal the final truth.
