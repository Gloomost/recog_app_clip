What can Storage Cleaner help you do?

1. Smart cleaning
Smart cleaning helps you search and filter related data such as picture information, address book information, calendar information, etc. You can manage multiple types of data at one time through the one-click cleaning function.

2. Picture management
Picture management can help you analyze similar or duplicate picture resources in the system album, and can also help you analyze screenshots and other resources, and you can choose to delete them.

3. Address book management
Address book management helps you analyze and filter duplicate contacts, similar contacts, invalid contacts and other information, and you can merge these contacts with one click.

4. Lock App
You can choose which application to lock in the Lock module, and then follow the tutorial instructions to create Personal Automation using the Shortcuts application. Then, you can lock the app you specify so that others cannot peek into the privacy of your app.

5. Internet speed test
It only takes one click, and everything is automatically produced. After a short time test, you can get the downlink speed and uplink speed of the current network.

6. Widgets
Desktop widgets allow you to add network tests, memory usage, CPU usage, etc. to your desktop.

7. Calendar management
Calendar management can make it easier for you to manage events added to the calendar in history. The app provides a simple operation method that allows you to delete unnecessary events very easily.

8. Mobile phone optimization guide
Here you can learn how to find unused apps, how to delete useless apps, and some common social software optimization methods.
