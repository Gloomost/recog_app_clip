Set in the enchanting Breadland, you will join courageous cookies on a quest to escape the clutches of a witch's oven. Together, you will build a thriving and beautiful kingdom of cookies, blending strength, strategy, and sweet success.Your mission is to construct a kingdom from scratch, transform barren lands into productive estates, and infuse your personal touch in designing and decorating a sanctuary for your cookie companions. Lead these brave cookies through unexplored territories, enhancing your collective might with each new challenge and adventure.Each cookie warrior possesses unique abilities and strengths, offering diverse strategies and tactics in battle. Your leadership and strategic choices will determine their success. Dive into the everyday life of cookies, be a part of dynamic building and production processes, and unravel thrilling stories and intense rivalries on this adventurous path. And watch out - a deep, dark conspiracy lurks beneath the surface, adding a twist to this sugary tale.Join us and unravel the secrets of this fantastical world of cookies!
