You can easily record, analyze and manage your blood pressure with just one app, Guard Blood Pressure!Main feature:1>Data record:You can enter the systolic blood pressure, diastolic blood pressure and heart rate data, and then select the measurement time to record, and finally view the data in the form of a chart or a list.2>Blood pressure trend analysis:According to the blood pressure and time data you input, we will automatically generate charts, the measurement data at different times are displayed in the same chart, and the recent blood pressure rise and fall changes can be seen at a glance!3>Information section:We have set up a special information section about blood pressure. By reading the information, you will learn some common knowledge about blood pressure and help you have a deeper understanding of your own condition.
