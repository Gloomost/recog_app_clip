Ngu Long Cuu Thien is a large-scale 3D fantasy mobile RPG game, based on the ancient book Son Hai Kinh, and takes the ancient wilderness as the setting. Players can play the role of descendants of clans. such as Anh Nhan, Cuong Dao, Linh Chuc, Than Xa, Kiem Quan, etc. Along with ancient gods such as Emperor Yan, Emperor, Phuc Hy, Nu Wa, etc., they travel in the wilderness and help the common people.
